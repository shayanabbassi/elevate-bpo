About Elevate BPO

A Call Partner Built Around One Metric: Your Close Rate

Most lead vendors sell volume. We sell conversations that convert. Elevate BPO combines trained call specialists, strict pre-qualification, and honest reporting so your agents spend their day closing — not chasing dead numbers.

  • Human-screened transfers — every caller speaks to a specialist before reaching you.
  • Dashboard you can trust — call recordings, durations, and outcomes in one view.
  • Fair billing rules — short calls, wrong numbers, and duplicates are never charged.
  • Support that answers — a real account manager, reachable seven days a week.
